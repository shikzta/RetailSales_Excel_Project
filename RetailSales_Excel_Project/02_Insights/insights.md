Monthly Sales Insights – Jan–Mar 2025


Key Findings

Laptops generate the most revenue : £37,250 (22.9% of Total).
West is the strongest region by generating a total of : £51,360.
Top 5 customers are David, Chris, Evan, Fatima and George in that order.  The laptop genereates 22.9% of all total revenue.  
Headphones are the worst-performing product; East is the weakest region.  
January and Feburary were strong months followed by a sharp drop in March. 
Not much data to show any trends, must wait to see for at least a year of sales.  

The Risks

High Dependancy on Laptop Product line (Nearly a quarter of all revenue)
Weak performance in East may indicate poor market penetration or low demand
Sharp decline in March sales could signal the start of a downward trend

Recommendations

Increase marketing focus in East and North to grow underperforming regions
Explore promotions or bundles for poorly performing products like headphones
Investigate March performance (pricing, seasonality, campaign changes) before it becomes a bigger problem
